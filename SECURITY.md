# Security

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Washtech Direct is committed to keeping our projects secure. If you discover a
potential security vulnerability, please report it **privately** to
[security@washtechdirect.com](mailto:security@washtechdirect.com).

**Do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly.**

### Guidelines for Reporting
* **Responsible Disclosure:** Do not share the vulnerability publicly without
  written permission.
* **Provide Details:** Include a clear description, steps to reproduce, and any
  proof-of-concept code.
* **Impact Assessment:** Help us understand the potential impact.

We value your efforts to help keep Washtech Direct and our community secure.
